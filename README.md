# 🌎 IA LIVE Foundation

## Global IA LIVE Movement for Lifelong Autonomous Learning

The IA LIVE Foundation promotes a new model of learning based on Artificial Intelligence, Living Works, and the Human Library.

Our purpose is to connect people, knowledge, technology, and nature to promote continuous learning throughout life.

## Our Vision

To create a global network where every person can learn, create, share knowledge, and leave a Living Work as evidence of their learning journey.

## IA LIVE Pillars

🤖 Responsible Artificial Intelligence

📚 Human Library

🌱 Health, Nature, and Well-being

🎓 Lifelong Autonomous Learning

🌎 Global Community

## Living Work

A Living Work is a practical creation developed by a person as a result of their learning process:

- digital projects
- research and innovation
- educational content
- social solutions
- conscious entrepreneurship
